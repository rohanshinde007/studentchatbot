About Us

Welcome to CHelper!

We are an AI-powered platform created to offer mental health and emotional support to students like you. Our mission is to provide a safe, non-judgmental space where you can share your thoughts and feelings, find helpful resources, and explore ways to manage stress and emotional challenges.

What We Offer:

  • A Listening Ear: We are here to listen to what's on your mind—whether it’s something personal or school-related, we are always ready to hear you out.
  • Information & Resources: We provide information on various mental health topics, coping mechanisms, and offer resources like hotlines and online support groups.
  • Practical Tips & Strategies: From stress management to building healthy coping mechanisms, we offer practical advice to help you manage your emotional well-being.
  • Encouragement for Self-Care: We remind you of the importance of taking care of yourself and guide you toward integrating self-care routines into your daily life.
